Super Stars FC is committed to providing a safe, respectful, and positive environment for all participants. This Code of Conduct applies to players, parents/guardians, coaches, staff, and spectators.

1. Player Conduct

  • Show respect to teammates, coaches, referees, and opponents.
  • Play fairly and follow the rules of the game.
  • Demonstrate good sportsmanship at all times.
  • No bullying, harassment, discrimination, or violence.
  • Arrive on time and be prepared for practices and games.
h2>2. Parent & Guardian Conduct
  • Support and encourage all players positively.
  • Respect coaching decisions and referee calls.
  • Refrain from yelling instructions from the sidelines.
  • Communicate concerns respectfully and privately.
  • Ensure player punctuality and readiness.

3. Coach & Staff Conduct

  • Provide a safe, inclusive, and supportive environment.
  • Lead by example with professionalism and integrity.
  • Teach skills, teamwork, and respect.
  • Never tolerate abuse or unsafe behavior.

4. Spectator Conduct

  • Cheer positively for all players.
  • No abusive language, gestures, or behavior.
  • Respect officials, coaches, and club staff.

5. Disciplinary Action

Violations of this Code may result in warnings, suspension, removal from activities, or permanent dismissal from Super Stars FC.

6. Acknowledgment

Participation in Super Stars FC activities constitutes agreement to abide by this Code of Conduct.
